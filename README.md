### 🧑‍💻 About Me

* ⚽ Passionate about football analytics, performance analysis, and the stories hidden in the data
* 📊 Building projects with football event data to explore shot quality, passing patterns, player performance, and team tactics
* 🧮 Interested in statistical modelling, expected goals (xG), match analysis, and data-driven decision making
* 🔭 Exploring player scouting through data visualisation, radar charts, clustering, and advanced football metrics
* 🎬 Analysing video and tagging matches using LongoMatch to connect what happens on the pitch with what appears in the data
* ✍️ Sharing my football analytics journey through articles and visualisations on [Medium](https://medium.com/@oskash6) and [Substack](https://substack.com/@oseko8)
